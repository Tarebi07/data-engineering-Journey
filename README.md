# Nigerian Car Market Analysis 🇳🇬

A data analysis project exploring the Nigerian car market using Python, Pandas and Matplotlib.

## What I analyzed
- Top 10 car brands in Nigeria
- Distribution of car prices
- Relationship between mileage and price
- Average car price by year of manufacture

## Key Insights
- Toyota dominates the Nigerian car market
- Median car price is ₦3.36 million
- Higher mileage = lower price
- Newer cars cost significantly more

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook
